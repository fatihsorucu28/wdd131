Site Plan: Turkey Travel Guide

Site Name

Turkey Travel Guide

Chosen because it clearly represents the purpose of providing travel information about Turkey.

Site Purpose

The Turkey Travel Guide website aims to provide comprehensive travel advice, highlight historical sites, offer cultural insights, and showcase popular travel destinations and cuisine in Turkey.

Scenarios

  • What are the top historical sites to visit in Istanbul?
  • Where can I find recommendations for traditional Turkish cuisine?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #007AFF (used for headings, buttons)

Secondary Color: #FF9500 (used for accents, links)

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text)

Secondary Font: Georgia, serif (used for headings)
